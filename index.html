RESEARCH LEADS

Pizzatti Patricia: Submitted FamilySearch pedigree Nov 2022. Likely a living relative from the Italian branch (Rocco or Abbondio's line).
James E. Vairetti: 1950 Census, Santa Barbara, CA. Born ~1890 Italy. Search passenger lists, naturalization records.
Vajeretti spelling: Search Ellis Island and passenger lists for VAJERETTI in addition to VAIRETTI.
Moreschi surname: Michele Sr.'s wife Margehrita Moreschi. New maternal line to research in Ponte in Valtellina.
Father name discrepancy: Mike's 1922 marriage record lists father as "Giovanni." Madeleine's 1933 record lists "Jacques." Jacques = Giacomo, which matches all other records. Giovanni likely a clerk error or Mike's confusion with English equivalents. Both list mother as Margaret Allera.
Ida Welzel timeline: Arrived S.S. Saxonia late Sept 1922. Married Mike 10 Oct 1922 (~2 weeks later). Czechoslovak citizen of German ethnicity, living in Switzerland. Listed as divorced on marriage record. Daughter of Franz Welzel & Ida Rotter. Strongly suggests pre-arranged marriage.
